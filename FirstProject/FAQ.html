Frequently Asked Questions


Forgot my password

If you've forgotten your Password, you can request to change it through the "Forgot Password" option on the Login screen.

Forgot my username

If you've forgotten your Username, you can request have it sent to you via email through the "Forgot Username" option on the Login screen.

Are the reviews real?

Yes! Belp only uses real, user generated reviews to ensure that you have authentic input for your next meal!

Where can I find local spots?

Belp will automatically give you a list of nearby restaurants according to your location. We love local spots, so we try and promote any you may not have tried yet!

What do the different price ranges indicate?

Each $ symbol indicates the average amount of money you can expect to spend for one person.
$ represents ~$10-20
$$ represents ~$30-50
$$$ represents ~$55<
Bare in mind this is only an estimate. If the prices are inaccurate when you dine in, please be sure to include it in your review!

How long will it take for an email back?

Our Customer Service Agents work hard to ensure that your needs are met as quickly as possible. You can expect a reply in 3-4 business days. We receive a lot of emails, so thank you for your patience!
